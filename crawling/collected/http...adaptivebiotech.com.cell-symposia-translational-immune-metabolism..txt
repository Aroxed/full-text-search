Symposia: Translational Immune Metabolism 06/24/2018 Cell Symposia:
Translational Immune Metabolism June 24-26 Basel, Switzerland During this Cell
Symposium, you will hear about the latest advances in immunometabolism from
physician-scientists, basic researchers and industry leaders. Topics will
focus on how local and systemic metabolism are integrated at the cellular
level to regulate immune cell function and how novel insights into
immunometabolism can be harnessed to advance and/or bolster therapeutic
interventions in metabolic diseases, inflammation, autoimmunity, and cancer.
