President and CEO By wmaster February 6, 2018 News No Comments 0 0 0
MARLBOROUGH, MA, February 6, 2018 – Adcole Corporation today announced that
its board of directors has appointed Jeff Walker as its President and Chief
Executive Officer, effective immediately. Jeff joined Adcole in 2017 as Vice
President of Global Sales and Marketing. An engineer by education, Jeff has
over 20 years of experience in sales, sales management, business development,
and marketing. Prior to joining Adcole he served as Vice President of Global
Sales and Marketing for Olympus Scientific Solutions Division’s analytical
instruments business unit. “Jeff has proven to be an effective leader since
joining Adcole and his skills will help sustain our position as a world leader
in industrial metrology while driving our strategic growth,” said Peter
Hunter, Chairman of the Board of Directors and Managing Director of the
private equity firm Artemis Capital Partners, majority owner of Adcole.
Commenting on his new role Adcole President and CEO, Jeff Walker, said: “I am
thrilled to be taking the leadership role for a company with Adcole’s rich
legacy. With over 60 years of history, Adcole is a true pioneer in precision
measuring gages for camshafts and crankshafts and possesses an exceptional
reputation for quality, accuracy, and reliability. I am excited most of all by
the new product innovations we are launching this year as well as those we
currently have in development. These new products promise to deliver
comprehensive metrology solutions to a wider range of customers by measuring
part features and geometries that were previously difficult or impossible to
do in the production environment. These new products will also help our
established automotive customers achieve higher quality and lower costs
through ground-breaking precision measurement.” About Adcole: Founded in 1957,
Adcole Corporation is a leading manufacturer of automatic and semi-automatic
measuring gages used in industrial metrology. Our purpose is to deliver the
highest quality gages with the highest level of measurement precision to help
our customers achieve their quality and manufacturing objectives.
​International space agencies, telecommunications companies, and the world’s
largest automotive manufacturers rely on Adcole products for mission-critical
applications. For more information on Adcole, please visit www.adcole.com.
About Artemis: Founded in 2010, Artemis Capital Partners is a Boston-based
private equity firm focused on acquiring and growing manufacturers of
differentiated industrial technologies, including specialty chemicals and
materials. Artemis seeks to partner with companies that have strong
established management teams, outstanding engineering capabilities, unique
products, and expanding niche markets. For more information on Artemis, please
